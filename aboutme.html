The Crep Cafe

The Crep Check was founded by Nicole Hughes. She built this idea from learning how to code. The objective was to create a website from scratch, and this is what she came up with.

The word 'crep', if you search for it on the Urban Dictionary, means 'trainers'.
The word 'cafe' just seemed fitting considering we're in a pandemic.

The initial idea was that it would've been a sneaker cafe, where people could sell their exclusive trainers to others who couldn't get a hold of them previously, for whatever reason. However, the idea swiftly changed into a clothing brand. Maybe in the future, both can be incorporated ;)
